# Rasna Experience — Mock Itinerary
## Group of 6 | October Olive Harvest | 5 Days / 4 Nights

---

**Guests:** 6 adults  
**Arrival / Departure:** Fiumicino Airport (FCO)  
**Season:** Mid-October (peak olive harvest, foliage at Lago di Vico)  
**Base:** Blera, Lazio — ~80 km north of Rome  
**All-inclusive price:** €1,500/person · **Total group revenue: €9,000**

---

## Lodging

### Option A — Beccone Albergo *(recommended)*
Historic albergo in the centro storico of Blera. Rooms above the restaurant — guests sleep, eat breakfast, and have dinner in the same building. Ideal for group cohesion. Partner restaurant means we can pre-set menus and control quality and cost. **3 double rooms, 4 nights.**

### Option B — Antonella's B&B *(alternative)*
Smaller, more intimate. Home-style hospitality, garden terrace, local family feel. Better if the group wants a quieter, more private base. Breakfast at the B&B; lunches and dinners at Beccone or out. **3 rooms, 4 nights.**

> **For this itinerary we use Beccone Albergo as base.** Meals on-site are marked **[Beccone]**; off-site venues are named individually.

> **Note on Ripa:** If there is a specific agriturismo or B&B called "Ripa" in or around Blera that has been identified as a potential partner, it should be scouted and added to this list — it may offer a rural/farm setting that fits the experience well.

### Day 2 — The Olive Harvest

**Lodging: Beccone Albergo**

| Time | Activity | Venue |
|------|----------|-------|
| 07:30 | Breakfast: cornetti, yogurt, frutta, caffè. | **Beccone Albergo** |
| 08:00 | Short drive or walk to the olive grove (5 min). Meet the landowner and family. Gloves and rakes distributed. | Olive grove, Blera territory |
| 08:15 | **Olive harvest begins.** Lay nets, rake by hand, sort and clean. Maria Grazia explains varieties, tree age, how yield varies by year. Midway break with bread, oil, water at 10:30. | Olive grove |
| 12:30 | Load crates. Drive to the **frantoio** (local oil press — Vetralla or Blera). Watch the full pressing cycle: washing, crushing, centrifuge separation. Taste fresh-pressed oil — green, peppery, intense. Each guest gets 1 bottle to take home. | Frantoio |
| 14:00 | **Field lunch** on a long table outdoors (or inside the mill). Pane casereccio, olio appena franto, sale grosso, formaggi, prosciutto, vino. Simple and honest. | At the frantoio / grove |
| 15:30 | Return to Beccone. Free afternoon. | Beccone Albergo |
| 17:00 | Optional: walk to the **Etruscan necropolis** at the base of the ravine (15 min on foot). Maria Grazia explains the Rasna name origin and the Etruscan civilization. Free. | Blera necropolis |
| 19:30 | **Dinner.** Acquacotta, tagliatelle al ragù, bistecca di manzo, patate arrosto, torta della nonna, vino della casa. | **Beccone Albergo** |
| 21:30 | Bar Etruria for a digestivo, then sleep. | Bar Etruria |

### Day 3 — The Artisans of Blera

**Lodging: Beccone Albergo**

| Time | Activity | Venue |
|------|----------|-------|
| 08:00 | Breakfast. | **Beccone Albergo** |
| 09:30 | Walk to **Renzo's leather workshop** in the village. Small space, smell of leather and wax, tools on every wall. | Renzo's bottega, Blera |
| 09:45 | **Hands-on leather session (3 hours).** Each guest chooses a project: small wallet, belt, or keyring. Renzo guides — cutting, hole-punching, waxed linen stitching, edge finishing. Maria Grazia translates. The object they make, they keep. | Renzo's bottega |
| 12:45 | Coffee break in the piazza. | **Bar Etruria** |
| 13:15 | **Lunch** — pasta fatta in casa, contorni di stagione, pane, acqua e vino. Hosted by Maria Grazia or a local family. Informal, seated around a table. | Maria Grazia's kitchen / local host |
| 15:00 | Visit to **Emiliano's** — tasting and guided explanation of his cured meats. How the animals are raised, the curing process, the role of salt and time. Full board: lonza, guanciale, coppa, salsiccia stagionata. Local wine to match each cut. | Emiliano's, Blera / surroundings |
| 16:30 | Each guest takes home a vacuum-packed selection (~200g). | Emiliano's |
| 17:00 | Return to Beccone. Free time. Sit on the terrace, read, nap. | Beccone Albergo |
| 19:30 | **Dinner** — lighter evening after the tasting. Zuppa di legumi, bruschette varie, tagliere di affettati (Emiliano's), frutta di stagione, cantucci e vin santo. | **Beccone Albergo** |

### Day 4 — Thermal Baths, Gardens & the Medieval City

**Lodging: Beccone Albergo**

| Time | Activity | Venue |
|------|----------|-------|
| 07:30 | Early breakfast. Pack swimwear. | **Beccone Albergo** |
| 08:30 | Depart by minivan toward Viterbo (~25 min). | Minivan |
| 09:00 | **Terme dei Papi** — the legendary outdoor thermal pool of Viterbo. A vast open-air pool fed by 58°C sulphurous spring water, cooled to ~40°C. October morning with steam rising, surrounded by medieval city walls. 2 hours in the water. Towels included. | Terme dei Papi, Viterbo |
| 11:00 | Change, dry off. Short drive to Bagnaia (~10 min). | Minivan |
| 11:15 | **Villa Lante, Bagnaia** — one of the finest Renaissance water gardens in Italy. Terraced fountains, cardinal's casino, cardinal's garden. Not crowded in October. ~1.5h guided walk with Maria Grazia. Entry €5/person. | Villa Lante, Bagnaia |
| 12:45 | Drive 10 min to Viterbo centro. | Minivan |
| 13:00 | **Lunch** at Trattoria del Richiastro — one of Viterbo's best traditional trattorias, in the medieval quarter. Lombrichelli all'amatriciana (local thick spaghetti), abbacchio al forno, carciofi alla romana, vino dei Castelli. | **Trattoria del Richiastro, Viterbo** |
| 14:30 | **Viterbo on foot** — walk the **Quartiere San Pellegrino**, the best-preserved medieval quarter in central Italy. External staircases, tower houses, narrow vicoli, logge. No admission fee. Maria Grazia leads the walk. | Viterbo centro storico |
| 16:00 | Drive south ~25 min to **Lago di Vico** — volcanic crater lake inside a nature reserve. Short lakeside trail through hazel and chestnut forest (October: peak foliage, hazelnuts on the ground). Snack by the water: fruit, nuts, local pastries. | Lago di Vico |
| 17:30 | Drive back to Blera (~20 min). | Minivan |
| 19:30 | **Dinner** — the group is tired in the best way. Pre-set menu focused on comfort: ribollita, pasta al sugo, porchetta, dolce. | **Beccone Albergo** |
| 21:00 | Final evening at Bar Etruria — aperitivo, amaro, say goodbye to any locals who drop by. | **Bar Etruria** |
