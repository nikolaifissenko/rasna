# Project Brief: RASNA / Blera Authentic Experiences

RASNA (named after the Etruscan word for "Etruscan people") is a pre-launch small-group immersive travel business based in **Blera, northern Lazio (Tuscia), Italy**. It offers 4-day/3-night all-inclusive packages (€1,400/guest, 6–10 guests) where international tourists participate in real seasonal agricultural, artisan, and cultural activities — not as sightseers but as temporary community members.

**Co-founders:** Nikolai + Maria Grazia (local partner with deep community relationships). Cristina Damiani's role TBD.

## Financial Model

- ~€680 margin/guest, ~€5,440 gross profit per 8-person group
- Deliberately capped at 15–20 groups/year to protect authenticity
- Break-even at ~2 groups
- Asset-light: partner BnBs, no owned property
