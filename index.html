Rasna

Harvest olives with local families. Hike through 2,800-year-old Etruscan tombs. Make your own leather bag with a village artisan. This isn't a tour — it's four days of doing the real thing.

Pick your adventure

"Rasna" is what the Etruscans called themselves — the civilization that carved cities into volcanic rock here nearly three millennia ago. Their tombs still line the ravines. Their roads still cut through the forest. You'll walk them yourself.

Why we stay small

This is not mass tourism.
