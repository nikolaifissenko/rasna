How Rasna works

Planning, experiencing, remembering.

San Giovenale by Donkey

Trek by donkey to the Etruscan ruins of San Giovenale, stopping along the way for panonto — a typical Bleran barbecue of bread roasted over the embers, drizzled with fresh local olive oil.

Il Mignone Hike

Tomb Raiding

Blera's tombaroli were real: for generations, locals dug through these necropoli by night, hunting Etruscan treasure long before archaeologists ever arrived. This is a scavenger hunt built on their story — it starts in the medieval village and leads you down into the tombs, following clues to track down what's hidden, with your host on hand to help along the way.

Year-round

Terrarte Sculpture Park

Wander Sandro Scarmiglia's open-air sculpture park, then stay for lunch in the olive grove or an aperitivo among the artwork.

Year-round

Pick-Up Football with Locals

Join the village for a casual game: no spectating, you're on the pitch with the people who live here.

Year-round

Festivals & Sagre

Il Pellegrinaggio

A springtime procession through Piazza Santa Maria on Easter Monday, one of Blera's oldest living traditions.

Easter Monday

L'Infiorata di Blera

For Corpus Domini, the whole town, kids, elders, everyone, lays elaborate flower-petal carpets through the streets for the procession.

Corpus Domini (May/Jun)

Festa di San Senzia

Blera's co-patron saint is honored with three days of traditional food and community celebration.

Jun 16 to 18

Coda alla Vaccinara & Porceddu

A weekend built around two hearty traditions: Roman oxtail stew and Sardinian roast suckling pig, cooked for the whole town.

Jul 24 to 26

Festa del Volontariato

A community festival run by Blera's volunteer associations, food, music, and the town out in force.

Aug 2 to 4

Stratto al Tartufo & Gnocco al Castrato

Handmade stratti pasta with truffle and gnocchi in castrated-lamb ragù, run by Blera's horse-lovers' association in the main square.

Aug 4 to 6

Fettuccine al Tartufo

In the hamlet of Civitella Cesi, long communal tables fill with truffle fettuccine, local meat, and live music.

Aug 11 to 14

Festa di Sant'Ermete

Blera's biggest weekend: mass and a relic procession for the patron saint, followed by fireworks, the town band, a livestock fair, ottava rima poetry, and the truffle and organic-produce festival.

Last Sun of Aug

Festa delle Cantine

Blera's wine cellars throw open their doors and everyone eats and drinks inside them, alongside tastings of the season's new wine and just-pressed olive oil.

2nd weekend of Nov
