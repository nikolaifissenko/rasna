Culture & Outdoors

Cammino dei Tre Villaggi

Italy's shortest cammino — a 20 km circular trail through Blera, Barbarano Romano, and Villa San Giovanni. Etruscan vie cave, tuff gorges, and the San Giuliano necropolis. You earn the "Etruscan Wanderer" certificate at the end.
