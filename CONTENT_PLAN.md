# Rasna — Content & Instagram Plan

Purpose: build visibility and proof *before* the pilot trip happens, so promotion doesn't start from zero. See `BUSINESS_PLAN.md` §8 for how this fits into the fall-launch contingency.

## 1. Account setup

- Handle: something like `@rasna.blera` or `@rasnaexperiences` (check availability) — keep "Rasna" as the brand name, consistent with `RASNA_PITCH_IT.md`.
- Switch to a Business/Creator account (unlocks insights + a link button).
- Bio: one line on the concept ("Live like a local in Etruscan Italy — small-group trips to Blera") + link to the landing page (nikolaifissenko.github.io/Blera-Business).
- Profile photo: something visual from Blera (a tomb, the town, olive groves) — not a logo yet.

## 2. Content pillars (rotate, don't repeat the same type back-to-back)

1. **Place** — Etruscan tombs, Via Clodia, countryside, town streets. Easy to shoot solo, no coordination needed.
2. **People/process** — Davide making cheese, Emiliano's charcuterie, Renzo's leatherwork, olive harvest. Ask Maria Grazia/contacts to send clips even when you're not on-site.
3. **Build-in-public** — the founder story of putting this together. Pre-launch, this is what earns trust before there's a finished product to show.
4. **Food** — home-cooked meals, panonto, sauce day — highly shareable, low effort to shoot.

## 3. Format

- Reels first — actively pushed to non-followers, which is how a zero-audience account gets discovered. Photos/carousels mostly reach people who already follow you.
- 15–30 sec, vertical, phone-shot, natural light, minimal editing — rough/authentic fits the brand better than polished.
- Daily Stories for raw behind-the-scenes between real posts.
- 2–3 Reels/week — consistency over volume.
- Hashtags: mix broad (#ItalyTravel #SlowTravel #AuthenticItaly) with niche (#Lazio #Etruscan #Tuscia). Geotag Blera/Lazio on every post.

## 4. First five Reels (post in this order, ~2–3 days apart)

### 1. Founder-story hook
- **Shots:** walking through Blera's streets/tombs, a few seconds of Maria Grazia, the countryside.
- **Text overlay:** "My mom's friend has lived her whole life in a 2,800-year-old Etruscan town. We're opening it up to a few travelers a year."
- **Caption:** "This is Blera — Lazio, Italy. Not Tuscany, not the coast. A town the Etruscans carved out of the tufo nearly 2,800 years ago, still living its own rhythm. We're building small, real trips here — no buses, no checklists. Following along as we put it together."
- **Tags:** #ItalyTravel #SlowTravel #Etruscan #Lazio #AuthenticItaly

### 2. Place — tombs / Via Clodia
- **Shots:** walking through the rock-cut tombs, the ancient road, wide landscape shots at golden hour.
- **Text overlay:** "Rock-cut tombs. A 2,000-year-old Roman road. No tour buses."
- **Caption:** "Via Clodia — the road the Romans built through Etruscan land. Most people have never heard of Blera. That's kind of the point."
- **Tags:** #Tuscia #ViaClodia #HiddenItaly #ItalyOffTheBeatenPath

### 3. Process/people — Davide's cheese
- **Shots:** Davide's hands working the cheese, tasting shots, the Civitella Cesi countryside.
- **Text overlay:** "Meet Davide. He's been making cheese and cured meat in Civitella Cesi his whole life."
- **Caption:** "This is the kind of thing you can't book on a normal tour — sitting with Davide while he shows you how it's actually made, then tasting it right there. This is what we mean by 'live it, don't just see it.'"
- **Tags:** #ItalianCheese #AgriturismoItalia #SlowFood #Lazio

### 4. Food — panonto / home cooking
- **Shots:** fire cooking, plates being served, hands passing food at a table.
- **Text overlay:** "Panonto — Bleran wood-fire cooking, made the way it's always been made."
- **Caption:** "Every meal on this trip is home-cooked, traditional, local. No restaurant menus. This is what 'cooked in the tradition of the nonne' actually looks like."
- **Tags:** #ItalianFood #FarmToTable #TraditionalItaly #CucinaItaliana

### 5. CTA — join the first group
- **Shots:** quick cuts from posts 1–4, ending on you or Maria Grazia looking at camera.
- **Text overlay:** "We're taking our first small group this fall."
- **Caption:** "Small groups. A handful of trips a year. Real people, real places, no script. If 'authentic Italy' without the crowds sounds like your kind of trip, link in bio — we'd love to have you in the first group."
- **Tags:** #ItalyTravel #SmallGroupTravel #AuthenticItaly #Blera

By the time post 5 lands, anyone who's watched the first four already has enough trust in the story for the ask to not feel cold.
