Group Dynamics — What to Watch For
One person dominates with localsRotate who asks questions. Brief the group before Renzo and Emiliano: "let him explain first, then ask."
Tension between two guestsDon't take sides. Free time every afternoon is built in — let people decompress independently.
Someone disengages from an activityAcknowledge it privately. Give them something useful: photos, carrying things, talking to the host. Don't make it a group moment.
The group goes quiet in a good wayStop. Don't narrate it or move on. Let it breathe. These are the moments that become testimonials.
Minor injury (blister, cut at Renzo's, twisted ankle)First aid kit in the van. Farmacia in Blera. Hospital: Ospedale di Viterbo, ~25 min drive.
What to Document During the Pilot
Photo of every activity: harvest, panonto fire, press, Renzo's hands, affettati board, gnocchi, terrace dinner
Short video: oil coming out of the press, the embers being lit, Renzo stitching leather
Note exact timings — when each session started and ended vs planned
Note any moment where energy dropped or someone disengaged
Note every moment where the group went quiet in a good way
Guest feedback form within 48h of return (5 questions: highlight? what to cut? would you recommend? what would you pay? one word for the trip?)
