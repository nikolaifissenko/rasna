RASNA
Authentic Experiences in Blera, Tuscia

Live it, don't just see it.

"Rasna" was what the Etruscans called themselves — the people who first carved this land out of the tufo, nearly 2,800 years ago. Blera still carries their mark: the tombs, the Via Clodia, the cliffs. We invite a small number of travelers each year to step into the real rhythm of this place — harvesting, cooking, walking among the Etruscan tombs, and sharing meals with the people who live this life every day.
