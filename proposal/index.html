Live the Tuscia, the way it has always been lived

Welcome to Blera

Rasna invites you into the real rhythm of life in the Tuscia hills — the work, the food, and the traditions that local families still carry on today. This is not a sightseeing tour. It's a few days spent alongside the people of Blera, doing what they do, eating what they grow, and learning crafts passed down for generations.

Below are the experiences available for your visit. Select the ones that interest you most — we'll build your itinerary around them.

Choose Your Experiences

A guided walk through the rock-cut Etruscan tombs and the ancient Via Clodia, tracing the roots of this land's first people — the Rasna.

An overview of the volcanic Lago di Vico, set within its own nature reserve, followed by a visit to the Renaissance Palazzo Farnese in Caprarola — one of Lazio's grandest villas.

Wander the San Pellegrino district, one of Italy's best-preserved medieval quarters, with its stone houses, outdoor staircases, and the Papal Palace.

Descend into the UNESCO-listed Etruscan painted tombs of Tarquinia, then explore the medieval town above, with its towers and views over the Maremma coast.
