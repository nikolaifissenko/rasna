## Scalability Note

| Group size | Revenue | Est. cost | Gross profit | Margin |
|------------|---------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| 6 guests | €9,000 | €6,513 | €2,487 | 27.6% |
| 8 guests | €12,000 | €7,513 | €4,487 | 37.4% |
| 10 guests | €15,000 | €8,513 | €6,487 | 43.2% |

*Fixed costs (lodging, Maria Grazia, transport, insurance) stay nearly flat as group size increases — each additional guest adds only ~€500 in marginal cost (meals, activities, takeaways), while adding €1,500 in revenue. **8 guests is the sweet spot; 10 is the ceiling for the authentic small-group feel.***
