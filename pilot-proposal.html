RASNA

This isn't just four days in Blera. It's an experience built around three stages — and unlike a typical tour company that plans everything for you, we plan it with you, have fun with you, and remember it with you.

1

Planning It — Together

Before arrival: a personal welcome, a call to shape the itinerary together, and a "what to expect" guide — the anticipation is part of the fun, and the plan is built together, not handed to you.

2

Doing It — Together

Four days of real, hands-on Etruscan life — workshops, meals with local families, hikes and the landscape around Blera — experienced side by side, not led from the front of a group.

3

Remembering It — Together

Afterwards: a shared photo/video recap, the recipes from the workshops, and a standing invitation back into the Blera circle — the story keeps being told together.

The Activities

Day Trips

Sample 4-Day Pilot Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Welcome

Transfer to Blera, welcome dinner with a local family, orientation walk through town together.

Day 2 — Land & Table

Hand-made pasta workshop in the morning, free time/exploration in the afternoon, dinner with what you cooked.

Day 3 — Artisans & Exploration

Morning with Emiliano (affettati) or a mosaic-making workshop, afternoon three villages hike, evening pizza-making.

Day 4 — Slow Morning & Departure

Traditional dessert-baking session, farewell brunch, recap conversation about the trip and what comes next, transfer out.
